About

The story behind Ledger

A personal project built to make budgeting clean, simple, and free for everyone.

Bakit Ledger?

Most budget trackers kasi ngayon ay either too complex at may features na hindi naman important, or may bayad... subscription sa madaling salita. So para makatulong sa taong bayan na mahilig gumastos at gustong ma-track yung expense nila eh, naisipan kong gawin itong Ledger — a clean, minimal tool that gives you full control of your finances without any cost. Libre ito, hindi ka namamalikmata.

With real-time charts, Google sign-in, and Firestore sync, Ledger offers a modern experience that works seamlessly across devices. Your data is always yours — stored securely and accessible anywhere.

Free Forever

No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Open-source and free to use.

Secure

Data protected by Firebase Authentication and encrypted Firestore.

Real-Time

Charts and summaries update instantly as you add transactions.

Responsive

Works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Meet the Developer

Ledger was designed and built as a personal project to explore modern web technologies while solving a real problem — keeping track of personal finances without the bloat.

Built using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with Firebase for authentication, Web3Forms for contact form and Firestore for real-time data sync. Chart.js powers the interactive visualizations.

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Firebase Firestore Web3Forms Chart.js
